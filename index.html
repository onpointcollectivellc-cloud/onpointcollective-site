The Collective

Institutional Rigor. Curatorial Precision.

On Point Collective LLC is defined by a singular mission: to strategically position conceptual art assets within sophisticated investment portfolios. We operate strictly as an advisory and brokerage firm, ensuring unbiased sourcing and valuation analysis. Our clientele consists exclusively of UHNW collectors, family offices, and investors seeking uncorrelated, tangible assets with measurable appreciation potential.

Every asset exceeding our $10,000 floor undergoes comprehensive due diligence covering provenance, artist trajectory, and secondary market liquidity. We bridge the critical gap between artistic merit and financial prudence.

Proprietary 70/30 Consignment Model

Equitable Alignment Between Investor and Brokerage.

Our foundational 70/30 Consignment Structure is proprietary and designed to create equitable alignment with the collector. This structure ensures that both the client (70%) and On Point Collective LLC (30%) are mutually incentivized for optimal asset performance and value realization.

This transparent, performance-driven approach is a departure from traditional gallery commissions, setting a new standard for institutional-grade art transactions.
